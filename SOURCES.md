## Verification Process

1. Cross-reference shop existence on Google Maps
2. Verify address and MRT station
3. Check rating on Google Maps (min 4.0 for inclusion)
4. Confirm broth type from shop's own social media or reviews
5. Price verified from recent reviews (within 6 months)
6. Wait time estimated from community reports

## Update Policy

- Core 39 shops: Verified quarterly
- Rankings: Updated annually after 台灣拉麵愛好會 vote (November)
- New shops: Added via PR with source URL required
- Closed shops: Marked as inactive after verification
